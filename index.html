Where you "Learn from the best"

Tech helpers is the online community which help students at University level by solving their issues in different technologies which they face during developement. Website is divided into different sections like forum section where anyone can post their queires, project ideas section where one can share project ideas, code or documentation upload section and so.

We will also provide good tutorials link time to time to broad the circle of learning. You can also use this website for storing your code and documentation by uploading it to our server. We provide 1GB space for our registered users. You can sign up on this site simply by giving your email address and password.
